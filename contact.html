Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process

How quickly can you start my project?

We can typically start new projects within 1-2 weeks after finalizing requirements and contracts. For urgent projects, we offer expedited onboarding.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages for all our projects, including bug fixes, updates, and feature enhancements.

What technologies do you specialize in?

We work with modern technologies including React, Node.js, Python, Flutter, React Native, AI/ML frameworks, and cloud platforms like AWS and Azure.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely! We're experienced in collaborating with in-house teams and can seamlessly integrate with your existing development processes.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, we're happy to sign Non-Disclosure Agreements to protect your confidential information and intellectual property.

What's your payment structure?

We typically work with milestone-based payments: 30% upfront, 40% at midpoint, and 30% upon completion. Custom arrangements are available for larger projects.
